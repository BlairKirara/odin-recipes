Mochi

Description

Mochi are a delicious japanese treat!

With this recipe you'll impress your friends and broaden your culinary horizons

  • 1 cup of rice flour
  • 1 cup of Azuki bean paste
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 of cornstarch
  1. Put red bean paste in a freezer until it solidifies
  2. Add water and sugar to the flour, stir it until mixed well
  3. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave for 3 minutes
  4. Divide red bean paste into 8 balls
  5. Stir the flour again and heat for about 30 seconds
  6. Put cornstarch on your workspace, then put two-table-spoon amount of flour on it
  7. Put red bean balls on top of the flour patches
  8. Pinch and press the dough until the ball is covered
  9. Sprinkle with additional cornstarch
