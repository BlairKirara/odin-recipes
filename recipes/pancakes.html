Pancakes

Description

Who doesn't love pancakes? Their fluffy texture makes your morning nice and precious.

With this recipe you'll be ready to start your mornings well!

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300 ml milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable or sunflower oil
  1. Pre-heat the pan
  2. Mix flour with eggs and milk
  3. After the mixture is ready pour some of it on the pan
  4. When one side is ready flip the pancake
  5. Repeat until you use up the mixture
