Pancakes

Description

Every single Sunday begins with pancakes… and ends with leftover pancakes from the morning. These are everything you want in a pancake.

They’re soft and so fluffy… each mouthful melts in your mouth… you’ll be reaching for more than one. Stack. Possibly.

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300 ml milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable or sunflower oil
  1. Pre-heat the pan
  2. Mix flour with eggs and milk
  3. After the mixture is ready pour some of it on the pan
  4. When one side is ready flip the pancake
  5. Repeat until you use up the mixture
